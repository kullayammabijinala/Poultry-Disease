Common Poultry Diseases

Coccidiosis

A parasitic disease affecting the intestinal tract, common in young poultry.

Fowl Pox

A viral disease causing lesions on unfeathered skin and mucous membranes.

Newcastle Disease

A highly contagious and fatal viral disease affecting respiratory, nervous, and digestive systems.

Avian Influenza

A viral infection that can cause mild to severe symptoms, and even sudden death.
